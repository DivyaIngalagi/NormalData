Hot Cocoa Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2T hot cocoa Milk
  • 8 sp milk
  • Marshmallows(OPTIONAL)

Method

  1. Scoop hot cocoa mix into mug
  2. Heat milk on the stove or in the microwave to just below a boil
  3. Pour milk into mug and stir gently as you pour
  4. Stir until mix is fully dissolve in the milk
  5. Top with marshmallows if you must
